Artificial intelligence is exactly what it sounds like it is--intelligence demonstrated by machines! Many AI machines, ranging from Siri to chatbots, are already used in daily life. We believe that everyone should have access to the powerful tools that AI provides. Diverse perspectives can output more accurate and innovative results that reflect society better as a whole. With the world being rapidly modernized, a diverse and inclusive workforce is crucial in order to produce powerful solutions. This is why we care.

Did you know that some types of artificial intelligence don't require coding at all? AI is largely based on statistics, probability, and data analysis. This makes it easy for anyone to learn AI fundamentals with no background in any programming language! One of Citro Tech's main goals is to make learning AI easy and fun.
