About

Citro Tech is a student-led organization dedicated to empower underrepresented communities in the tech field and provide resources to increase awareness and diversity in artificial intelligence. üçã

Here at Citro Tech, we care about diversity in STEM and AI fields. We know how intimidating it can be for those interested in pursuing a career in tech but are too scared to because of the lack of representation in their own community. We strive to ‚Äãcreate a welcoming and inclusive community to support our fellow peers who are curious about these topics.

Artificial intelligence is exactly what it sounds like it is--intelligence demonstrated by machines! Many AI machines, ranging from Siri to chatbots, are already used in daily life. We believe that everyone should have access to the powerful tools that AI provides. Diverse perspectives can output more accurate and innovative results that reflect society better as a whole. With the world being rapidly modernized, a diverse and inclusive workforce is crucial in order to produce powerful solutions. This is why we care.

Citro Tech has 4 sub-teams: Blog Writers, Content Creators, Project Developers, and Outreach. We work on articles, short videos, and research projects with insightful info. We also publish a newsletter every 2 weeks with internship opportunities, learning resources, and event updates. Lastly, our grand plan is Citro Hacks, an all-inclusive and unforgettable hackathon coming in 2021!

Did you know that some types of artificial intelligence don't require coding at all? AI is largely based on statistics, probability, and data analysis. This makes it easy for anyone to learn AI fundamentals with no background in any programming language! One of Citro Tech's main goals is to make learning AI easy and fun.
