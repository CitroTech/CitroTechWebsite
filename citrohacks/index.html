About

Citro Hacks is an all-inclusive online hackathon organized by Citro Tech, a student led organization dedicated to empowering minorities in the tech field. We want to provide a valuable experience for both beginners and advanced coders alike. We have plenty of swag, workshops, and games for attendees to enjoy at no cost. Join us to meet new friends, network with computer science professionals, and build something awesome!

FAQ

A hackathon is an event where many programmers gather up for a couple hours to collaborate on a project, network with people, get free stuff, and have fun!

Citro Hacks is open to all high schoolers from grades 9 through 12 and recently graduated seniors. If you are in college or beyond, you can register as a mentor!

All you need is a computer, an internet connection, and curiosity! It'd be helpful to have some food and a comfortable workspace too.

Citro Hacks is 100% Free! Thanks to our generous sponsors, we are able to cover all costs for swag and shipping.

Don't worry! We have plenty of beginner-friendly workshops lined up to help you build your first app/website! Plus, our mentors will be active in our Discord server, helping everyone out.

You may form a team with up to 4 members, but going solo is cool too! You can message our #find-a-team channel in our Discord server, and we'll also host an team mixer after the opening ceremony!

Fill out the signup form to register, Join our Discord server so you don't miss any announcements, and Join the Devpost to submit your project!

We will gladly answer any questions you have! You can contact us here.
